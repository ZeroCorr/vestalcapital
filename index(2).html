Our Focus

Capital Meets Clean Energy Infrastructure

Vestal Capital Management identifies, acquires, and manages high-quality wind energy assets across proven corridors. We bring a disciplined, institutional approach to alternative energy — combining deep sector knowledge with rigorous financial analysis to deliver enduring value.

As a principal investor, long-term lessee, and direct landowner, we participate across every layer of the wind turbine value chain. From greenfield project development to the acquisition of operating assets, our firm is positioned to capture opportunity wherever it exists in the wind energy ecosystem.

Investor

Deploying private capital into wind turbine assets with proven cash flows and long-term contracted revenue.

Lessee

Structuring and managing long-term wind turbine lease agreements that benefit all stakeholders in the project lifecycle.

Landowner

Acquiring and developing strategic land positions in high-capacity wind corridors for long-term infrastructure value.
